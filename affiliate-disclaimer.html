Affiliate Disclaimer

Transparency is important to us. This page explains our affiliate relationships and how we earn commissions from qualifying purchases.

1. What Is an Affiliate Program?

An affiliate program is a marketing arrangement where an online retailer pays commission to external websites for traffic or sales generated from its referrals. When you click on an affiliate link on our website and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

2. Our Participation in Affiliate Programs

Syrian Flavors participates in several affiliate programs, including:

  • Amazon Associates Program: As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases made through our affiliate links to Amazon.com.
  • Cooking Equipment Retailers: We may earn commissions when you purchase cooking equipment through our links to various kitchen supply retailers.
  • Specialty Food Suppliers: We may receive compensation when you purchase specialty ingredients through our recommended suppliers.
  • Book Publishers: We may earn commissions when you purchase cookbooks through our links to online booksellers.

3. How Affiliate Links Work

When you see a product or service mentioned on our website with a link, it may be an affiliate link. These links have special tracking codes that identify us as the referring source. If you click on one of these links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission.

Important: The price you pay for products or services is the same whether you use our affiliate links or go directly to the retailer's website. The retailer pays us a commission for referring you.

4. Our Commitment to You

We are committed to maintaining your trust and providing honest recommendations. Our affiliate relationships do not influence our content in the following ways:

  • We only recommend products and services that we genuinely believe will benefit our readers.
  • We provide honest and unbiased reviews of products and services.
  • We clearly disclose when a link is an affiliate link.
  • We do not allow affiliate relationships to influence the topics we cover or the opinions we express.

5. Product Reviews and Recommendations

When we review or recommend products on our website, we strive to provide honest and thorough evaluations. Our primary goal is to help you make informed decisions about the products and services that will enhance your Syrian cooking experience.

6. Amazon Associates Disclosure

Syrian Flavors is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

7. Changes to Our Affiliate Relationships

We may add, change, or remove affiliate relationships from time to time. This disclaimer will be updated to reflect any significant changes in our affiliate practices.
