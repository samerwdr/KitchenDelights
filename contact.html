Frequently Asked Questions

Can I submit my own Syrian recipes?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our community and sharing authentic family recipes. Please use the contact form above to reach out with your recipe, photos, and any special story behind it. Our team will review it and potentially feature it on our blog.

Do you offer cooking classes or workshops?

Yes! We periodically offer both online and in-person Syrian cooking workshops. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified about upcoming classes. You can also request a private cooking class for special events or corporate team building activities.

Where can I find authentic Syrian ingredients?

Many Middle Eastern grocery stores carry authentic Syrian ingredients. We've also compiled a list of online retailers where you can purchase specialty items. Check our resources page for more information, or feel free to contact us for specific ingredient recommendations.

Can I request a specific Syrian recipe?

Of course! We're always looking for new recipe ideas. If there's a particular Syrian dish you'd like to see featured on our blog, please let us know through the contact form. We'll do our best to research and develop the recipe for you.
